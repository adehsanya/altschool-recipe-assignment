Nigerian Jollof Rice

A delicious West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, peppers, and spices. This recipe is easy, flavorful, and perfect for family gatherings!

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 45 minutes
  • Servings: 4 people
  • Difficulty: Medium

Dish Image

Ingredients

  • 3 cups long-grain rice
  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • Salt and seasoning cubes
  • 1 tsp curry powder

Instructions

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and half of the onion.
  2. Saut√© chopped onions in oil and add tomato paste.
  3. Pour in the blended mixture and fry until thick.
  4. Add chicken stock, seasoning, curry, and salt.
  5. Add the rice, cover, and cook until soft.
  6. Reduce heat and steam the rice to finish.

Tips & Notes

For best results, use long-grain parboiled rice so the grains stay firm and separate.
